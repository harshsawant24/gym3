DIET ROUTINE

Support your fitness journey with our recommended daily meal breakdown.

Breakfast

  • Oats with berries & almond milk
  • 2 boiled eggs or tofu scramble

Mid-Morning Snack

  • Greek yogurt with honey or fruit
  • A handful of almonds or walnuts

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken or chickpea salad
  • Brown rice or quinoa and steamed veggies

Evening Snack

  • Fruit smoothie or protein shake
  • Whole-grain toast with avocado

Dinner

  • Salmon or paneer with roasted vegetables
  • Sweet potato or whole wheat roti

Before Bed

  • Warm milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • A few almonds or pumpkin seeds
